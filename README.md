## Energy Auditing Overview
### Purpose
The purpose of this energy audit was to systematically evaluate the facility's energy consumption patterns, identify energy inefficiencies, quantify potential savings opportunities, and develop a practical roadmap for reducing energy costs while improving operational sustainability.

### Scope of Work
The audit covered
- Electrical energy consumption analysis
- Building and facility energy assessment
- Lighting systems
- HVAC and ventilation systems (where applicable)
- Office equipment and appliances
- Production and operational equipment
- Water heating systems
- Renewable energy opportunities
- Energy management practices and employee behaviors

### Expected Outcomes
The audit will provide:
- Comprehensive energy consumption baseline
- Identification of major energy-consuming assets
- List of Energy Conservation Opportunities (ECOs)
- Estimated energy and cost savings
- Carbon emission reduction estimates
- Investment requirements
- Implementation roadmap
- Monitoring and verification plan
